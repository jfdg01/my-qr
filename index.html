How to get my info?

To use the generator, you need your Card Number and your Device ID.

Step 1: Log out

On the device that normally generates QR codes, log out of the gym app.

Step 2: Login page

Open the login page using the "Login" button.

Step 3: Copy the popup URL

Copy the URL from the login popup by long-pressing it and selecting "Copy".

Step 4: Paste the URL

• Option A (Easy): Paste the full URL directly into the "Login via URL" field.

• Option B (Advanced): Paste the URL elsewhere to manually extract deviceID and card-Number.
